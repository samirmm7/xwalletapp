Privacy Policy – XWallet - Finance Control

Last updated: 10/30/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the XWallet - Finance Control handles user information.
We value your privacy and want to be transparent about how your data is managed.

1. Data collection and storage

[App Name] does not collect, store, or share any personal user data on external servers.
All information entered in the app (such as values, descriptions, categories, and financial transactions) is stored only on the user’s device.

2. Use of information

Your data is used solely for local app functionality, allowing you to:

Record income and expenses;

View reports and balances;

Manage your personal finances easily.

No information is transmitted to the internet, servers, or third parties.

3. Device permissions

The app may request basic permissions (such as local storage) only to save and read data within the device itself.
These permissions are used exclusively for the app’s internal operations.

4. Data security

Since all information remains on the user’s device, data security depends directly on access control to the device (e.g., password, biometrics, etc.).
